Cookie Policy

Last updated: 13 June 2026

This Cookie Policy explains how the Prayer Reminder website and web app, operated by Red Face (Pty) Ltd, use cookies and similar local storage technologies.

1. What cookies and local storage are

Cookies are small text files stored on your device. The web app also uses browser local storage to remember your preferences and keep you signed in. Together these help the app function and remember your settings.

2. How we use them

  • Essential: to keep you signed in, maintain your session, and remember settings such as your chosen faith, reading-plan progress and notification preferences.
  • Functional: to store data locally so the app works smoothly and remembers your content on your device.

3. What we do not do

We do not use cookies to sell your data or to build advertising profiles. We do not run third-party advertising trackers in the app.

4. Managing cookies

You can clear or block cookies and local storage through your browser settings. Note that disabling essential storage may prevent sign-in and stop some features from working correctly.

5. Changes

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date reflects the latest revision.
