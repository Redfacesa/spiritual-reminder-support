Refund & Cancellation Policy

Last updated: 13 June 2026

This policy explains how subscriptions, cancellations and refunds work for the optional Pro plan in the Prayer Reminder app, operated by Red Face (Pty) Ltd.

1. Free to use

Prayer Reminder is free to download and the core features are free forever. A paid Pro subscription is entirely optional and unlocks advanced features such as expanded AI guidance and sermon transcription summaries.

2. Billing

  • Pro is billed on a recurring basis (for example monthly) through our payment processor, Paystack, or through the relevant app store.
  • Your subscription renews automatically at the end of each billing period unless you cancel beforehand.
  • Applicable taxes may be added depending on your region.

3. Cancelling your subscription

You can cancel at any time:

  • App Store (iOS): Settings → your name → Subscriptions → Prayer Reminder → Cancel.
  • Google Play (Android): Play Store → Profile → Payments & subscriptions → Subscriptions → Prayer Reminder → Cancel.
  • Paystack subscriptions: contact us at redfacesa@gmail.com and we will cancel future renewals.

After cancellation, you keep Pro access until the end of the period you have already paid for.

4. Refunds

  • Purchases made through the Apple App Store or Google Play are subject to those stores' refund policies, and refunds are handled by Apple or Google.
  • For payments made directly through Paystack, you may request a refund within 14 days of a charge if you have not substantially used the Pro features in that period.
  • We may decline refund requests that appear fraudulent or abusive.

5. How to request a refund

Email redfacesa@gmail.com with your account email and the payment reference. We aim to respond within 5 business days.

6. Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date reflects the latest revision.
