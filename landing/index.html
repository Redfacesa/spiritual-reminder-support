Good to know

Frequently asked questions

Is Prayer Reminder free?

Yes. The app is free to download and use. An optional Pro subscription unlocks advanced AI features such as unlimited guidance and sermon transcription summaries.

Which faiths are supported?

Christianity, Islam, Judaism, Hinduism, Buddhism and a universal/spiritual track. Content and daily verses adapt to the tradition you choose.

Do I need an account?

No. You can use the app fully without signing in. Creating an optional account lets you securely sync your prayers, saved verses and reading-plan progress across devices.

Is my data private?

Absolutely. We don't sell your data. Read our Privacy Policy for full details on what is stored and how it's protected.

Which devices are supported?

Prayer Reminder is available on iOS via the App Store and on Android via Google Play.
