Data & Account Deletion

Last updated: 13 June 2026

We respect your right to control your data. This page explains how to delete your Prayer Reminder account and associated data. Prayer Reminder is operated by Red Face (Pty) Ltd.

1. Using the app without an account

If you never signed in, your prayers, saved verses, reading-plan progress and notes are stored only on your device. Simply uninstalling the app removes that local data.

2. Delete your account and synced data

If you created an account to sync across devices, you can request full deletion in two ways:

  • In the app: open Profile → Settings → Account and sign out, then email us to request deletion of synced data.
  • By email: send a request from your account email to support@prayerreminder.site with the subject "Delete my account".

3. What gets deleted

Upon a verified request we permanently delete the data associated with your account, including:

  • Your profile and authentication record (email).
  • Prayer requests, prayer logs and saved verses.
  • AI conversation history and usage records.
  • Sermon notes, transcripts and summaries.
  • Subscription and payment references we hold (where retention is not legally required).

4. Timeline

We process deletion requests within 30 days. Some records may be retained longer only where required by law (for example, financial/tax records related to payments), after which they are deleted.
