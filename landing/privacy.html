Privacy Policy

Last updated: 12 June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Red Face (Pty) Ltd ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses and protects your information when you use the Prayer Reminder mobile application and related services (the "App"). By using the App, you agree to the practices described below.

1. Our privacy-first approach

Prayer Reminder is designed to work locally on your device. You can use the core features — prayer reminders, sacred texts, daily inspiration and reading plans — without creating an account or sharing personal information. We do not sell your personal data to anyone.

2. Information you provide

  • Account information (optional): If you choose to create an account to sync your data across devices, we collect your email address and authentication details.
  • Spiritual content you create: Prayer requests, saved verses, reading-plan progress, sermon notes and similar content you enter in the App.
  • AI conversations: Messages you send to the in-app AI guide so we can generate a response.
  • Audio recordings (optional): If you use sermon recording and transcription, the audio you record is processed to produce a text transcript.

3. Information collected automatically

  • Device & usage data: Basic technical information such as device type and app version, used to keep the App stable and secure.
  • Notifications: Reminder schedules are stored on your device to deliver local notifications at the times you choose.

4. How we use your information

  • To provide reminders, content and features you request.
  • To sync your data securely across your devices when you sign in.
  • To generate AI spiritual guidance and sermon transcriptions/summaries.
  • To process optional Pro subscription payments.
  • To maintain, protect and improve the App.

5. Third-party service providers

We use trusted providers strictly to operate the App. They process data only as needed to deliver their service:

  • Supabase — secure cloud database and authentication for optional account sync.
  • xAI (Grok) — processes AI guidance requests and speech-to-text transcription. Requests are routed through our secure server so your device never holds our AI keys.
  • Paystack — processes Pro subscription payments. We do not store your full card details; these are handled by Paystack.

6. Data retention

Content stored only on your device remains until you delete it or uninstall the App. If you have an account, your synced data is retained until you delete it or request account deletion. You may request deletion at any time by emailing us.

7. Your rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to access, correct, export or delete your personal data, and to withdraw consent. To exercise these rights, contact us at redfacesa@gmail.com.

8. Children's privacy

The App is rated for everyone and is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us so we can remove it.

9. Security

We use industry-standard measures, including encryption in transit, to protect your information. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, but we work hard to safeguard your data.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above and, where appropriate, through in-app notice.
